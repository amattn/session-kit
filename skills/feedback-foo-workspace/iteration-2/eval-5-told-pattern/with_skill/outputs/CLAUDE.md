## NOTES.md Discipline

**Update NOTES.md after every decision, before moving to the next topic.**

After completing any decision (approve, reject, rename, choose between options):
1. Stop — do not proceed to the next topic
2. Write the decision and rationale to NOTES.md
3. Only then continue

If you realize you skipped a NOTES.md update, stop immediately and catch up
before doing anything else. Do not accumulate debt.
